Metro Business College

The METRO BUSINESS COLLEGE is an academic institution established in 1993 in the Republic of the Philippines, accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), Department of Education (DepEd). The institution aims to provide a high-quality education to both local and foreign students who are seeking for a solid foundation in management and business.
Since its establishment, the MBC managed to rise and become one of the country's premier business colleges and is incessant in putting effort to continuously improve the administrative services and operational processes of the institution.
